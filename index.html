ABOUT THE BAND

April Company is an indie band from Houston, TX founded in the fall of 2018. Coming from a variety of musical backgrounds and influences, it's their unique contrast of sounds that allows April Company to stand out, fusing upbeat energy with alternative rock, soothing lyricism, and the lo-fi musicality of bedroom pop.
